First to 10 wins!


In Tennis, a game completely different to Pong, try to beat your opponent by hitting the ball past them with a paddle to score points.


Single Player: Use the arrow or W/S keys to move
Multiplayer: Use the arrow keys for the green paddle and W/S keys for the red paddle
